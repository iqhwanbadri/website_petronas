Executive (Data Science)


Location: Wilayah Persekutuan Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia


Job Description

The chosen applicant will apply their knowledge of data science to determine which models are most appropriate for solving business problems, create thorough solutions for data science projects, and implement these solutions in real-world setting. To work well with stakeholders across the firm, the ideal candidate should also have outstanding communication skills.


Requirements

  • A bachelor's degree in engineering, computer science, data science, or a equivalent field.
  • Three years or more experience managing data science initiatives.
  • Expertise in Python, particularly familiarity with algorithms and data structures.
  • Proficiency in statistical analysis, modeling, and exploratory data analysis (EDA).
  • Prior expertise creating and implementing machine learning models.
  • Preferable proficiency in at least one of the following fields: computer vision, deep learning, text analytics, optimization, or simulation.
  • Prior knowledge of reinforcement learning or generative AI is advantageous.
  • Excellent critical thinking and problem-solving abilities.

If you are interested in this open positions, please submit your resume and cover letter at careers@petronas.com.
