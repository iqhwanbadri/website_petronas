Senior Manager (Software Engineering)


Location: Sabah, Malaysia


Job Description

The Software Engineering Senior Manager (Technical Role) is the pinnacle of the technical career path. Candidates for this position are expected to possess exceptional expertise, demostrate strong leadership, and have a significant impact on the organization.


Requirements

  • Recommended qualifications include a degree in Software Engineering, Information Systems, Computer Science, Data Engineering, or a related Digital Engineering field, with a minimum of 12 years of experience in software development and delivery.
  • At least 6 years of technical management experience, with a proven track record of leading software engineering teams and delivering digital products at scale.
  • Expert knowledge in the following technology domains: infrastructure, data centers, network, security, disaster recovery, system integration, cloud, and monitoring.
  • At least 10 years of hands-on experience in web/mobile development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, responsive design).
  • At least 10 years of hands-on experience with various programming stacks (.Net/C#/WebAPI, Angular; Node.js/React.js/Javascript, Java, Kotlin, Swift, Objective C).
  • At least 10 years of experience in database design (e.g., MS SQL, MySQL, PostgreSQL, SQLite, Couchbase, Realm), including T-SQL.
  • At least 10 years of experience in designing and developing middleware services (API) and ETL processing.
  • At least 5 years of experience in agile development methodologies (e.g., Scrum, Kanban, Lean) and security frameworks and governance (e.g., OWASP, SonarCloud, Fortify).
  • At least 5 years of experience in cloud development and architecture (using MS Azure or Amazon Web Services).
