Internship

Internships for Students

Eligibility Requirements

  1. Only citizens of Malaysia may apply.
  2. Applicants must be enrolled in undergraduate programs in subjects related to PETRONAS at domestic or international universities where an internship is required as part of the program.
  3. Applicants must be enrolled in diploma programs at domestic or international universities where internship is required, preferably in technical or engineering fields pertinent to PETRONAS.
  4. The internship has to last for eight weeks at the minimum and eight months at the maximum.
  5. Email submissions of the aforementioned documents are required; incomplete submissions will not be taken into consideration.
  • Resume
  • A copy of Mini Transcript
  • A copy of the university's letter regarding industrial training or internship, if applicable.

T&C

  1. Applications must be submitted three months prior to the start date of the internship and are accepted based on availability.
  2. Only those who are selected for an internship will be notified. Please consider your application unsuccessful, within two months after you submitted it, but receive no response.
  3. PETRONAS ensures that only the most qualified and eligible individuals are selected for internship positions by offering placements based on merit and equal access for all.
