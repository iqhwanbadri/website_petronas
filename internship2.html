Internship in Engineering Department, GPU for July 2024

Location: Kemaman, Terengganu, Malaysia

Job Description

Background: Degrees in Computer Science, Electrical/Electronics, and Instrumentation/Control systems.

The candidate would supervise different technological projects and efforts in GPU while collaborating with a team of engineers in the Engineering Department. The candidate must use different engineering theories in real-world settings.

Applicants are required to:

  • Produce a technical report on engineering activities.
  • Participate at meetings and record minutes.
  • Support and keep an eye on site operations.
  • Take part in the different departmental initiatives.

If you are interested in any of the internship programs, please submit your Resume, Mini Transcript, and University's Letter at careers@petronas.com.
